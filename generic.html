Price Prediction from Weather Data

Technical University of Denmark (DTU)

The goal of this team project was to develop ARIMA, SARIMAX, and a temporal Convolutional Neural Network (tCNN) to predict electricity spot prices in Denmark, and then comparing those models. We used CentralPower (MWh), LocalPower (MWh), and OffshoreWind (MWMWh) as the hyperparameters to test the models. This was part of a project I worked on for my Energy Analytics class I took during my semester abroad.
